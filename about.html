Meet the Animator

Bringing characters to life through passion and precision

Obioma

I'm a passionate 2D animator with 7 years of experience creating engaging animations that tell stories and connect with audiences. My journey began at Babcock University where I studied Computer Science, blending technical knowledge with artistic creativity.

Specializing in character animation, rigging, and motion design, I bring a unique perspective to every project. My work combines technical precision with artistic flair, resulting in animations that are both visually stunning and emotionally resonant.

When I'm not animating, you can find me sketching new characters, studying animation techniques, or mentoring aspiring animators in my community.
